# Phase 3 F2 Image and Rollout Remediation

- Local perimeter variation, rather than one corner pixel, is required to distinguish sprite contrast from gradient, grid, and noise backgrounds.
- Promotion receipts are canonical SHA-256 artifacts. A later stage validates the prior receipt hash, its approval/stage, and each retained output-artifact hash.
- A frozen selection must bind the pairing manifest byte hash and complete selected pairing rows, not only pair IDs.
- Missing assessment artifacts are rejected with a persisted receipt; they must never leak `FileNotFoundError`.
